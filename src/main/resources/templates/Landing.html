How It Works

No account needed. Three steps from zero to competing.

01

Open the Form Link

Admin shares a unique registration link for each event. Open it — no login, no signup, just the form.

→
02

Fill Your Details

Enter your name, email, college, and participation type. Pay the fee and upload the payment screenshot.

→
03

Get Code by Email

After admin verifies your payment, your unique QR code and participant code is sent to your email instantly.

🎯 On Event Day — Show your code to the judge. They enter your marks. Results are published after the event.
